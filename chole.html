Chole-Bhature:

Punjabi Chole Bhature is not just food; it’s one of the most tempting delicacies to provide ultimate satisfaction to your taste buds. After just one bite of chole bhature, we’re certain you won’t be able to resist yourself to have more. If you have ever been to the North-Western region of India or lived in Delhi, you must be aware of their spicy and mouth-watering cuisines that are hard to resist Besides, it can even be enjoyed at lunch or dinner. There’s no specific time to eat chole bhature for Punjabis

Ingredients

For-chole

  1. Chickpeas
  2. Oil
  3. Salt
  4. Turmeric Powder
  5. Chilli-Powder
  6. Garam-Masala
  7. Chopped-Vegetables
  8. Lime-Juice
  9. Tea-Bag

For-Bhature

  1. Refined Flour
  2. Wheat Flour
  3. Oil
  4. Yeast
  5. Water

Procedure:

For-chole

  • In a pan add oil,Chopped vegetables
  • After vegetables get browned add turmeric powder,Chilli powder,Salt fry it
  • For de-glazing the pan, add a little water.
  • Now, add the chole (soaked overnight and pressure cooked) to the masala.
  • Now, add ajwain, chopped green chilies and water for the base.
  • To get the color in the chole, add a tea bag to the masala.
  • Add lime juice and a dollop of butter to it.
  • Garnish the chole with coriander and butter and serve them hot with bhaturas

For-Bhature

  • Knead the whole wheat flour, maida and salt together with adequate amount of water.
  • Sprinkle the yeast on top. Leave it for 2-3 hours for the yeast to work
  • Divide it into equal portions. Roll out in an oval or round shape.
  • Deep fry till golden brown, like a puri. Serve and enjoy!
